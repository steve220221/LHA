A Historic Milestone

In 2029, Lamoni will celebrate 150 years since its founding in 1879

Our Foundation

Lamoni was established in 1879 as a planned community, designed to embody ideals of cooperation, education, and spiritual growth. From its carefully laid-out streets to its emphasis on learning and culture, Lamoni represented a unique vision for community life in Iowa.

The town quickly became known for its progressive approach to education, establishing Graceland College (now Graceland University) and fostering a culture of intellectual curiosity that continues to this day.

Celebrating Our Journey

The Sesquicentennial celebration will honor both our founding principles and the remarkable journey of growth, adaptation, and resilience that has brought us to 2029. From agricultural innovation to educational excellence, from small-town values to global connections, Lamoni's story is one of continuous evolution while maintaining core values.

This milestone celebration will bring together residents, alumni, historians, and friends of Lamoni from around the world to commemorate our past and envision our future.

Opening ceremonies, historical exhibits featuring founding families, restoration of historic buildings, and the launch of the Sesquicentennial time capsule project. Special focus on the vision and sacrifice of Lamoni's founders.

The grand Sesquicentennial Festival featuring historical reenactments, traditional crafts demonstrations, musical performances, and a community-wide reunion. Culminating in the official anniversary weekend celebration.
